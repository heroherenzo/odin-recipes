Maple Pecan Blondies

These maple pecan blondies are perfect for pecan lovers. With a brownie like texture, they are chewy and have crunchy edges.

These warm and cozy maple pecan blondies have a brownie-like texture with fudgy with crunchy edges, according to recipe tester Juliana Hale.
